The letter had been there all along, of course. She had written it to herself the night before, in the careful block capitals she always used when she wanted to be sure she would believe it later.

*Do not trust the man in the gray coat.*

She read it twice, folded it back into her pocket, and walked downstairs to the kitchen, where the man in the gray coat was already pouring her coffee.
