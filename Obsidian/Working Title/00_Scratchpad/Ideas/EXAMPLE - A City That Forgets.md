# A City That Forgets

> This is an example idea note. Delete it when you start using the vault.

A city where every resident wakes up with the prior day erased from memory. Relationships, jobs, enmities — all rebuilt from physical artifacts: letters pinned to walls, photos, notebooks. What happens to someone who refuses to leave notes? Who benefits from the blank slate?

## Seeds
- A detective who solves a murder they themselves committed the day before.
- An annual festival where people deliberately burn their notes.

## Possible shape
Short story, first-person, present tense.
